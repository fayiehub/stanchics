Our Story

A community still being written — by us.

We held our first meeting in early 2026 — a room full of brilliant women working across engineering, product, data, and design. What struck us wasn't just the talent in that room, but the hunger for connection.

Stanchics is still finding its shape, and that's exactly the point. You get to help build this.

Where community comes to life

Talks & Panels

Conversations led by women in tech on the topics that actually matter to our careers and communities.

Meetups
