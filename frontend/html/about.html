Our Story

It started with one room and a lot of recognition.

In early 2026, a group of women working across Kenya's tech industry sat down together for the first time. There was no formal agenda. No keynote. Just an honest conversation about what it means to be a woman building a career in this space.

What emerged from that conversation was a shared hunger: for community that goes deeper than LinkedIn connections, for spaces where we can talk about the real stuff, and for a network that lifts as it climbs.

Stanchics is that space. It's new, it's growing, and it belongs to the women who show up for it.

What We Stand For

Our values

01
Community First

Every decision we make is measured against one question: does this serve the community? Not the brand, not the optics — the people.

02
Authenticity

We show up as we are. No performance, no polished façade. The truest connections happen when we're honest about our experiences.

03
Lifting As We Climb

Success in this community is measured by how many people we bring with us. Mentorship, referrals, amplification — it's all part of the work.

04
Inclusive Excellence

We hold space for women across every discipline, background, and career stage in tech. Diversity of thought is our greatest strength.
