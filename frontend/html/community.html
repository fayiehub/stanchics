Community Guidelines

How we show up for each other

"Stanchics is a space built on trust, warmth, and the belief that we are all better when we lift each other. Here's how we keep it that way."

Show up generously. Share knowledge, make introductions, and celebrate wins loudly — your own and others'.

Speak honestly. We value real conversations over polished performances. Vulnerability is welcome here.

Respect every woman's journey. Career paths are not linear. No comparison, no judgment — only curiosity and encouragement.

Keep the space safe. What's shared in our community stays in our community unless someone explicitly shares otherwise.

Lift as you climb. The measure of this community isn't who rises to the top, but how many we bring with us.
